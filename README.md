Petty Cash Management System – A Full Stack Web Application for Managing 
Organizational Cash Expenses 


2⃣ Introduction 
The Petty Cash Management System (PCMS) is a web-based application designed to help 
organizations manage and track small day-to-day cash expenses efficiently. 
Traditionally, petty cash is managed manually using notebooks or spreadsheets. This often 
leads to: 
● Missing receipts 
● Calculation mistakes 
● Lack of transparency 
● Delays in approvals 
This system digitizes the entire process using modern web technologies. 


3⃣ Aim of the Project 
To develop a secure and efficient web-based system that automates the management, tracking, 
and reporting of petty cash transactions within an organization. 


4⃣ Objectives 
● To eliminate manual record-keeping of petty cash. 
● To provide real-time tracking of cash balance. 
● To implement a structured approval workflow. 
● To maintain transparency in expense handling. 
● To generate reports for auditing and analysis. 
● To ensure secure role-based access control. 


5⃣ Goals 
● Improve accountability in small cash transactions. 
● Reduce errors in expense calculations. 
● Provide digital storage for receipts. 
● Enable quick approval and reimbursement. 
● Maintain proper financial records for auditing.
